Frequently Asked Questions

Is online therapy secure?

Yes, all our video sessions are encrypted end-to-end to ensure your privacy and confidentiality. We use a secure platform that is compliant with all professional regulations.

How much does a session cost?

The cost per session is determined by each professional. You can see the prices on their individual profiles. We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and PayPal.

What if I don't connect with my therapist?

Finding the right fit is crucial. If your first session doesn't meet your expectations, we encourage you to browse other profiles until you find a professional you feel comfortable with. There's no obligation to continue.
